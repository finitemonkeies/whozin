# Whozin Investor One-Pager

## What Whozin Is

Whozin is a private social layer for nights out. Instead of asking users to browse endless event inventory, it helps them answer the question that actually drives attendance:

**Who is going, and is this the move?**

Whozin combines event discovery, lightweight RSVP, and social proof into a single product experience built for concerts, club nights, festivals, and friend-group coordination.

## The Problem

Most event products are optimized for listings, not decisions.

- Ticketing platforms show inventory, not social momentum
- Social platforms show people, but not dependable event intent
- Group chats are noisy, fragmented, and hard to act on
- Users often decide late because they do not know who else is going

The emotional gap is simple: people do not just want events. They want confidence.

## The Product Insight

Attendance is social. Conversion often happens when people see:

- friends going
- momentum building
- a night that feels worth showing up for

Whozin turns that hidden signal into a product.

## Product Experience

Whozin is built around three core loops:

### 1. See Who's Going

The app foregrounds friend presence, avatar stacks, counts, and recency signals instead of burying them behind logistics.

### 2. Act Fast

RSVP, login, and setup flows are designed to be low-friction and one-tap wherever possible.

### 3. Know The Move

The product introduces "The Move" as a recommendation layer that elevates socially relevant events, making the product feel predictive instead of passive.

## Why It Can Matter

Whozin sits between ticketing, social discovery, and friend coordination, but its wedge is sharper than any one of those categories alone:

- It is not a generic event marketplace
- It is not a public social network
- It is not a planning tool

It is a decision engine for going out.

## Brand Positioning

Whozin presents as:

- private by default
- socially intelligent
- nightlife-native
- premium and selective

That positioning matters because social nightlife behavior is identity-sensitive. The product cannot feel loud, cheap, or overexposed.

## Why The Brand Works

The current product brand signals the right market instincts:

- dark, nightlife-native visual language
- strong social-proof-first messaging
- privacy reassurance near conversion points
- high-contrast, action-oriented UX
- editorial recommendation language via "The Move"

The result is a product that feels closer to insider access than mass-market listings.

## The Whozin Wedge

If ticketing platforms answer "Can I buy this?"
and event marketplaces answer "What exists?"
Whozin answers:

**"Should I go, and who will be there?"**

That is a stronger emotional use case than discovery alone.

## User Promise

Whozin helps users:

- find the right nights faster
- reduce social uncertainty
- act on momentum instead of guesswork
- keep visibility and privacy under control

## Strategic Takeaway

Whozin's brand and product already align around a differentiated story:

**social proof for nights out**

That is a credible wedge into event behavior because it targets the real conversion trigger, not just the listing layer.

## Short Investor Narrative

Whozin is building the private social layer for live events. The product makes event decisions easier by surfacing the signal users care about most, who is going, then turning that signal into action through fast RSVP, lightweight coordination, and momentum-based recommendations. It looks and feels native to nightlife culture rather than generic ticketing, which gives the brand a sharper right-to-win in socially driven attendance.
