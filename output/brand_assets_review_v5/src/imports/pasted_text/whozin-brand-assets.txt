Create a polished brand asset system for a mobile nightlife app called Whozin.

Brand summary:
Whozin helps you see who is going before you go.
It is the private social layer for nights out and live events.

Core brand feeling:
dark, immersive, nightlife-native, private by default, high-signal, selective, social-first, premium, modern, energetic without chaos

Audience:
social event-goers, nightlife crews, concert/club/festival audiences, friend groups deciding where to go based on who else is going

What the brand should communicate:
social proof first
private confidence
late-night energy
fast decisions
premium but accessible
not a generic event marketplace

Visual direction:
Use a dark-mode-first visual system with deep black and zinc neutrals as the base, then controlled bursts of saturated pink, purple, and subtle blue glow.
The look should feel like club posters, neon haze, smoked glass, blurred ambient light, and backstage social access.
Keep it clean and high-contrast. Avoid clutter, cheesy party tropes, or loud festival flyer chaos.

Color palette:
Black: #000000
Canvas: #09090B
Surface: #18181B
Elevated surface: #27272A
Primary text: #FFFFFF
Secondary text: #A1A1AA
Muted text: #71717A
Hot pink: #DB2777
Electric purple: #9333EA
Signal blue: #3B82F6
Violet: #A855F7
Success green: #22C55E
Rose: #F43F5E
Orange: #F97316

Primary gradients:
Brand CTA gradient: linear-gradient(to right, #DB2777, #9333EA)
Icon gradient: linear-gradient(135deg, #EC4899, #9333EA)

Shape language:
rounded square geometry
premium, not cute
generous corner radius
soft but structured
glassmorphism and smoked acrylic surfaces
subtle translucent borders
ambient glow and blur behind elements

Typography direction:
bold, compact sans-serif headlines
clean, neutral sans-serif UI text
tight tracking for headlines
wide tracking for micro-labels, pills, and status tags
tone is confident, warm, short, and in-the-know

Logo direction:
Build the primary Whozin mark as a rounded-square gradient tile with a bold white W glyph centered inside it.
The W should be simple, geometric, highly legible at small sizes, and feel direct and premium.
No outlines, no bevels, no chrome, no 3D gimmicks.
Create:
1. icon-only mark
2. horizontal lockup with icon + wordmark
3. stacked lockup
4. one-color dark version
5. one-color light version

Asset set to create:
1. App icon system
- iOS / Android app icon concept
- favicon concept
- square social avatar version
- monochrome small-size version
- safe-area and small-scale legibility checks

2. Social media profile assets
- Instagram profile icon
- TikTok profile icon
- X profile icon
- Spotify / event-partner friendly avatar variant
All should feel consistent and highly recognizable at tiny sizes.

3. Social share system
- Open Graph / Twitter card, 1200x630
- Instagram story promo frame, 1080x1920
- square social announcement post, 1080x1080
Use the messaging:
“See who’s going before you go.”
Optional supporting line:
“The private social layer for nights out.”

4. Brand toolkit
- color swatches
- gradient recipes
- logo clear space rules
- icon spacing and radius specs
- sample background treatments
- social-proof-inspired badges or chips
- a “Private by default” pill
- a “The Move” badge style

Important stylistic constraints:
Lead with people and social momentum, not ticketing or logistics.
This should feel selective and alive, not mass-market.
Avoid generic event app visuals, loud EDM clichés, neon overload, emojis, confetti, crowds, or cheesy nightlife stock imagery.
Color should be controlled: mostly dark neutrals with focused accent glow.
Make the system feel premium, minimal, and instantly brandable.

Output frames:
- Brand overview board
- Logo exploration board
- Final logo system board
- App icon presentation board
- Social profile icon board
- Social card mockups board
- Mini style guide board

Final deliverable goal:
A cohesive asset family that makes Whozin feel like a private, premium nightlife social product with strong recognition across app icons, profile icons, and social media surfaces.
