Whozin brand essence:

The brand should feel:
- private but electric
- premium but accessible
- social but not noisy
- nightlife-native but not trashy
- confident but not corporate
- youthful but not immature

Overall visual direction:
- dark foundation
- black, ink, charcoal, graphite surfaces
- controlled signature accents in hot pink, fuchsia, violet, and electric purple
- premium glow, blur, and translucency used intentionally
- cinematic, selective, high-signal energy
- never generic SaaS
- never crypto, gamer, EDM flyer, or template-like
